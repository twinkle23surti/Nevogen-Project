O&M Scope

What Our O&M Programme Covers

Our solar operations and maintenance programme is built around six integrated service pillars. Together they ensure reliable generation, proactive fault management, regulatory compliance, and long-term protection of your solar energy assets.

Preventive

Scheduled Maintenance

Calendar-based servicing of modules, inverters, structures, cabling and switchgear. Every visit includes detailed inspection and performance verification to ensure long-term system reliability.

Module cleaning Inverter checks Thermal scans
Corrective

Fault Response

Rapid diagnosis and resolution of inverter trips, string faults, protection relay issues and cabling failures with defined SLA response times to minimize downtime.

4-hr critical SLA String isolation Component replacement
Monitoring

Remote SCADA Monitoring

Continuous monitoring of generation output, inverter parameters and string-level performance. Automated alerts enable immediate fault detection and investigation.

24/7 generation data String visibility Automated alerts
Reporting

Performance Reporting

Monthly performance reports covering generation trends, PR and CUF metrics, loss analysis and maintenance activities to ensure full transparency of system performance.

PR tracking Loss analysis Annual review

CEIG inspections Safety audits Compliance docs
