Our Process

End-to-End Engineering Process

A disciplined, stage-gated approach that ensures zero surprises at commissioning and protects performance throughout the asset life.

01

Site Assessment

Detailed survey, structural analysis, shadow study, grid feasibility and load profiling — foundation of every project.

02

System Design

PVSyst-based yield modeling, SLD preparation, component selection and layout optimization tailored to site reality.

03

Regulatory Approvals

Complete handling of CEIG/SEIG clearance, DISCOM approvals, net metering and open access documentation.

04

Procurement & Logistics

Tier-1 sourcing with full traceability, quality checks and timely delivery to site — no compromises.

05

Field Execution

Supervised civil, structural, electrical and mechanical installation by certified teams — quality at every step.

06

Commissioning & Handover

PR testing, system handover, monitoring setup and detailed documentation — performance locked in from day one.
